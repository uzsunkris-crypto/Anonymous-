About Richsea Wallet

Richsea Wallet was built to give everyone — from beginners to power users — a secure, private and easy-to-use way to manage crypto assets. We are committed to financial freedom, strong security practices, and returning control to users by building a non-custodial wallet that’s both powerful and approachable.

Our core values: Security, Privacy, Simplicity — built on transparent policies and continuous improvement. Richsea supports multi-chain access, swaps, conversions and earning opportunities so your crypto works for you.

Quick Facts
Non-custodial: You control private keys & funds.
Multi-chain: Support for popular networks and tokens.
Privacy-first: Manage data and app permissions.

Privacy Policy for Richsea Wallet

1. Introduction
Richsea Wallet (“we”, “us”, “our”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our website and mobile application (“App” / “Service”).

2. Information We Collect
- Personal information you provide: e.g. email address, name (only if voluntarily submitted), and contact information for support.
- Device and usage data: IP address, device type, OS, unique identifiers, and anonymous analytics (if enabled).
- Transaction data: blockchain addresses and transaction details necessary to perform wallet functions. We do not collect private keys.

3. How We Use Your Information
- To provide and maintain the Service (wallet operations, account management and support).
- To improve app performance, features and user experience.
- To send important updates and notifications (only if you opt-in).
- To maintain security and prevent fraud (e.g., logs used for troubleshooting and protection).

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure
We do NOT sell personal information. We may disclose information if required by law or to comply with legal processes. Third-party service providers (e.g., analytics or hosting) that help operate the Service may process limited data on our behalf under contractual protections.

5. Data Security
We use industry-standard security measures—such as encryption and access controls—to protect your data. Richsea Wallet never transmits or stores your private keys on our servers. Always keep your recovery phrase and private keys secure.

6. Your Rights
You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. You can opt out of non-essential data collection and communications at any time by contacting us.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy as our Service evolves. We will post the revised policy on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
